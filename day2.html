Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu

Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu (born 9 August 1975) is an Indian actor, producer and philanthropist who works in Telugu cinema. He is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema and has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2012.[1][2] He has appeared in over 25 films and is a recipient of several accolades including, nine Nandi Awards, five Filmfare Awards South, four SIIMA Awards and two Gaddar Telangana Film Awards.
